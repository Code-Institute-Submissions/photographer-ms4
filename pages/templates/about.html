ABOUT ME

The photographic work with artists and musicians of Brazilian music was the beginning of everything, where I was able to have wonderful experiences with fantastic people.

These are some of the names which are part of my portfolio and work done:
Luan Santana, Roberta Miranda, Pablo, Zezé di Camargo and Luciano, Fernando and Sorocaba, Jorge and Mateus, Lucas Lucco, Maiara and Maraísa, Simone and Simária, Tuca Fernandes, Rick and Renner, Ivete Sangalo, Dj Dennis, Alok, Vintage Culture , Make u Sweat, Jet Lag, Bell Marques, Chitãozinho and Xororó, Babi Muniz, Wesley Safadão, Double You, Katinguelê, Inner Circle, Alpha Blondy, Anitta, Planet Hemp, Melim, Saulo, Daniela Mercury, among others.

I have worked in countless festivals and events around Brazil such as:

Salvador Summer Festival in the years 2018, 2019 and 2020, Villa Mix Festival, VIP Girl, As Patroas, Campus Party, Flica year 2017, among others, including DVD recording, as official photographer, photo operator and video taken aerials by drone.

One of my passions is photography work in the studio. I have produced numerous advertising campaigns for Brazilian artists, restaurants and photographic models in my studio.

I have direct action in the control of lighting, photography and production always focusing on achieving a result with unique personality and soul.

I could not forget to mention that I sign several menus for restaurants and bistros in São Paulo and the Northeast of Brazil, photographing dishes and recipes that are true works of art.
When talking about works of art, I have a collection of photographs for Fine Art, which allows me to work directly with architects and decorators. In the year 2019, I worked at the events Casas Conceito and Casa Cor da Bahia - Brazil.

This is just a summary of more than eleven years of dedication and passion for what I do, and I hope to contribute even more to the segment in the coming years.
